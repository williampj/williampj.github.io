Most recently I co-created BridgeAPI, a serverless integration platform that lets you connect your apps through event-driven workflows

bridgeapi

BridgeAPI is serverless app that empowers users to collect all their webhooks in one place and chain them to forwarding API requests, thereby establishing event-driven workflows across apps with BridgeAPI as the proxy.
Compared to existing solutions, BridgeAPI is advantaged by being open source, easy to setup, and equipped to both receive and forward http requests.
